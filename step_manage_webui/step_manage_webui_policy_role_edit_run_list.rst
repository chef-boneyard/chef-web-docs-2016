To edit the run-list for a role:

#. Open **Chef Manage**.
#. Click |webui policy|.
#. Click |webui policy_roles|.
#. Select a role.
#. Click |webui policy_roles_edit_run_list|.

   .. image:: ../../images/step_manage_webui_policy_role_edit_run_list.png

#. Make your changes.
#. Click |webui button_save_run_list|.
