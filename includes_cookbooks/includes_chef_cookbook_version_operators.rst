The following operators can be used with cookbook versions:

.. list-table::
   :widths: 200 300
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Operator
     - Description
   * - =
     - Equal to
   * - >
     - Greater than
   * - <
     - Less than
   * - >=
     - Greater than or equal to
   * - =<
     - Less than or equal to
   * - ~>
     - Approximately greater than
